About Digital Literacy

Digital literacy encompasses the skills needed to live, learn, and work in a society where communication and access to information is increasingly through digital technologies like internet platforms, social media, and mobile devices.

Our course is designed to help individuals of all ages develop the knowledge and skills to navigate the digital world confidently and safely.
